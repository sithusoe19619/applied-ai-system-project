# Kidney Diet and Hydration Support

Pets on kidney-support diets often need strict consistency around meals and hydration. Wet-food feeding schedules, water refreshes, and appetite monitoring are common routine tasks that owners can track day by day.

Owners should follow the veterinary feeding plan already prescribed and avoid changing food or supplement amounts without veterinary guidance. If a pet refuses multiple meals or shows sudden decline, the safe action is to contact a veterinarian.

Care plans for kidney-support diets should avoid vague feeding advice and instead focus on reminders, monitoring, and documenting whether the prescribed routine was completed.
